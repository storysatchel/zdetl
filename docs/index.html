Hello, I am GM Satchel, a lifelong TTRPG aficionado; although when it comes to Traveller, I've yet to make it out of character creation!

Zdetl is the official, standardized language of the Zhodani Consulate of the Traveller universe. Its structure was first codified in Zhodani year 300, five thousand years before the founding of the Third Imperium, and has remained relatively stable in grammar and construction ever since.

This website is the result of my attempt to expand--without countermanding!--the lexicon cribbed from many, many Traveller sources over the years. I am doing this using a tool called audition, designed to facilitate "rapid, interactive iteration on conlang syntaxes, morphologies, and lexicons". What audition does that's really grand is parse glosses (eg attack#IMP) into your conlang by applying your derivational morphologies (#IMP -> u'de') to your lexicon ("attack" -> kiatl)!
